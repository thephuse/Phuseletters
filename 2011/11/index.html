Wire Transfer Issues

We’ve been having a few problems with wire transfers from some clients in the US that we’ve just recently solved. We apologize for the confusion. The problem was that more detailed information needed to be provided to American banks. It has been a learning experience, but we think we’ve sorted it all out now.

We know that wire transfers are not the ideal situation, but for now they beat out the alternatives. We’re still looking into payment gateways, but have yet to find one that matches the needs of our clients in a way that won’t break the bank. If you have any better ideas for alternatives, we’d love to hear them. For now, we’ll continue to use wire transfers, and will also allow cheques to be sent in via courier (e.g. FedEx, UPS, Purolator).
