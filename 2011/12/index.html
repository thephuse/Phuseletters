2011 in Review

2011 was a lot of things. Exciting, different, a “S#!tshow”, as Esquire magazine so aptly put it. Between natural disasters and man-made revolutions, there was a lot to take in. And yet, despite the chaos whirling around us, The Phuse managed to set records in clients, in projects, in employment, and in revenue—unprecedented records.

And thanks to you lovely people, yes YOU reading this, we’re optimistic about breaking the records of last year and setting brand spanking new ones in 2012.

The Phuse’s Goals for 2012

We plan to bring more people on staff as needed to compensate for the increasing amount of work we’re receiving without any loss in the quality of our designs or the standards of our coding. We also want to offer bigger and better incentives to our team to keep the amazing talent we have.

Highlighting the importance of content strategy and the user experience in our work is a developing process, one which we plan to improve upon greatly in the coming year. We believe our projects will all benefit from better planning and greater attention to details.

And we haven’t forgotten about you! We’re going to plan more customer appreciation giveaways to make sure you know how much we value you!
