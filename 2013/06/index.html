Hey Phusers,

This month has been a real treat. Not only did we have 3 birthdays at The Phuse (Flip, Jenna and James! Happy birthday, you’re all wonderful!), but we’ve had the pleasure of working on great projects, and getting busy to the point where we are actually looking for a new UI/UX designer to join the team!

If you want to be notified and were not included in the mailing list last month, let Matt know by emailing him at matt@thephuse.com .
